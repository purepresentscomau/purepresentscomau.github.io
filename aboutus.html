About Us

Pure Presents is a touring and events company primarily focusing on touring artists who represent the current Hip-Hop/Rap culture as well as creating unique event, festival & lifestyle experiences. We operate across Australia, New Zealand and Asia.
Established in 2017 hosting end of exam parties, we are now hosting our first show in 2019 working with the artist Mickey Singh for the biggest south Asian tour this decade. Our team is made up of a dedicated, music loving, passionate group of like-minded individuals who strive for success working across a diverse group of artists, which we love.
For further information about what we do please contact us.
