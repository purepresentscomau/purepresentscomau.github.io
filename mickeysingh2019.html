Mickey Singh April 2019


Urban Asian icon Mickey Singh will be bringing his headline World Tour to our shores during the Easter festive break; certainly being the biggest South-Asian production to this day.
Hailing from Los Angeles, California, Mickey has always had a broad crossover appeal with his pop-R&B approach to Punjabi music thus paving his own lane in this industry.
From the launch of his career with ‘Mickstape’ in 2013 to his recent debut EP ‘Magic’ (2018), Mickey Singh has had a successful stint within the Urban Desi niche genre and will continue to dominate during his prime.
He will be showcasing his very recent Urban Desi Musical & ‘MAGIC’ EP with top of the chart hit singles 'Yarri Yeah' and 'YTL'. Mickey will also be taking us down memory lane with his classics Phone, In Love, Bad Girl, Double Addi & many more.
Supporting him will be his official DJ/producer DJ ICE as well as Punjabi Trap artist, PAM SENGH recognised from his track Karenga Aayi as well as his fuego assist on Fateh’s track Chakkme.
Also supporting will be the finest DJs and MCs in the continent holding it down for these unforgettable nights.
As tickets to the concert productions will be limited, fans are encouraged to act quickly to secure their tickets for an intimate night with the King of Urban Desi himself, Mickey Singh!
