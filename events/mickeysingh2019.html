Mickey Singh April 2019

Urban Asian icon Mickey Singh will be touching down in Australia/New Zealand during the festive season of Easter for the biggest urban tour this decade.
We touching base in ADELAIDE, BRISBANE, SYDNEY, MELBOURNE, PERTH & AUCKLAND for 6 litty shows that’ll definitely take a spot in your insta highlight reel.
Hailing from Los Angeles, California, Mickey has always had a broad crossover appeal with his pop-R&B approach to Punjabi music which has allowed him to pave his own lane in this industry making him an untouchable artist.
From the launch of his career with ‘Mickstape’ in 2013 to his recent extended play ‘Magic’ (2018), Mickey Singh has been on top of the Urban Desi game and is not looking to step down anytime soon.
He will be showcasing his very recent E.P, ‘MAGIC’ with top of the chart hits such as Yarri Yeah and YTL. Mickey will also be taking us on a ride with his classics Phone, Rooftop Party, Bad Girl, Double Addi & many more.
Supporting him will be his official DJ/producer DJ ICE as well as PAM SENGH who is a Punjabi Trapper well known from his track Karenga Aayi as well as his fire verse on Fateh’s track Chakkme.
Also supporting will be the finest DJs and MCs in the continent holding it down for these unforgettable nights.
These shows are going to go off, so fans are encouraged to act quickly to secure their tickets for a night with the king of Urban Desi himself, Mickey Singh!
